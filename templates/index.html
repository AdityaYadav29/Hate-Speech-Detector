What is a hateSpeech detector?

It detects public speech that expresses hate or encourages violence towards a person or group based on something such as race, religion, sex, or sexual orientation.

This web app is powered with ML, NLP which identifies whether the message is a hate speech or offensive speech or just normal speech.
